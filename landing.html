The problem

Security teams do not lack information. They lack prioritization.

Most teams already monitor too many feeds, too many sources, and too many alerts. The real challenge is not collecting more data. It is identifying which threats deserve attention now.

SecPrior helps reduce that noise by turning fragmented cyber monitoring into structured, actionable priorities for defenders.

Why SecPrior

Built for teams who need signal, not more dashboards

SecPrior is designed for SecOps, CTI, and blue team workflows where time, context, and prioritization matter.

Context-aware prioritization

Surface the threats that deserve attention first using severity, exploitation context, article correlation, and operational relevance.

CVE and incident correlation

Connect cyber news, vulnerabilities, and incident patterns in one workflow so analysts understand what is happening and why it matters.

Analyst-ready briefings

Generate concise outputs for analysts and readable summaries for managers without forcing teams to sift through fragmented feeds.

Watchlists and exposure visibility

Track vendors, technologies, assets, and strategic topics that matter to your environment.

Operational sharing

Export results, trigger notifications, and plug outputs into existing workflows through email, webhooks, and integrations.

Self-hostable and open source

Deploy in your own environment, review the code, adapt the workflow, and contribute improvements back to the project.
